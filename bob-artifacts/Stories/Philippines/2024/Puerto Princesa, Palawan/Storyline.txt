Puerto Princesa Adventure — The Gateway to Palawan’s Wonders

Before reaching the more famous islands of Palawan, the journey started in Puerto Princesa — the city known as the gateway to the province’s natural treasures.

Unlike the island adventures that followed, Puerto Princesa had a different kind of charm. It was a mix of city life, nature, and quiet coastal moments — a place where you could slow down while still being surrounded by beautiful landscapes.

One of the highlights of the trip was exploring the Puerto Princesa Underground River, one of Palawan’s most iconic destinations. Entering the cave felt like stepping into another world — surrounded by massive rock formations, mysterious chambers, and the sound of nature echoing inside. It was a reminder of how incredible and powerful nature can be.

Beyond the famous attractions, Puerto Princesa was also about enjoying the simple moments — discovering local food, appreciating the peaceful surroundings, and taking in the beauty of Palawan at a slower pace.

This stop became the perfect beginning to a bigger Palawan adventure. A place that prepared the way for the beaches of El Nido and the untouched paradise of Balabac.

Sometimes the journey isn’t only about the final destination — it’s about the places that become part of the story along the way.

Puerto Princesa — where the Palawan adventure begins. 🌴✨
